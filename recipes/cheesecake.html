Easy No-Bake Cheesecake

Description

Easy No-Bake Cheesecake is a creamy, delightful dessert that requires no oven, making it perfect for any occasion. Its silky texture and rich flavor come from a blend of cream cheese, sugar, and whipped topping, all nestled on a crumbly graham cracker crust. With minimal ingredients and steps, this cheesecake is quick to prepare and ideal for beginners. Itâ€™s versatile enough to customize with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, chocolate drizzle, or caramel sauce, ensuring every slice is as indulgent as the last.

Ingredients

  • 2 8 oz packs cream cheese, room temperature
  • 14 oz. sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 Tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 prepared pie crust cookie crust
  • Topping fresh fruits, canned cherries, etc

Instructions

  1. Beat cream cheese and sweetened condensed milk on medium speed until smooth and creamy.
  2. Beat in lemon juice and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour cream cheese mixture into prepared pie crust.
  4. Refrigerate for 3 hours or until firm.
  5. Add topping when ready to serve.
