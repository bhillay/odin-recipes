3-Ingredient Buttermilk Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self-rising flour
  • 1 stick frozen butter (1/2 cup) + more for brushing
  • 1 cup cold buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. (Read tips in notes before proceeding.)
  2. Add flour into a large bowl.
  3. Grate the frozen butter into the flour.
  4. Using a pastry cutter, cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  5. Pour in the cold buttermilk and stir with a rubber spatula until a dough forms.
  6. Lightly flour a work surface and turn the dough out onto it.
  7. Flour your hands and gently knead the dough, adding more flour as needed. The dough should be slightly sticky.
  8. Fold the dough several times to create layers. Work quickly to keep the dough cold.
  9. Pat the dough into a 1-inch thick rectangle.
  10. Using a 3-inch biscuit cutter, cut out 4 biscuits. Regather and pat the remaining dough to cut more biscuits.
  11. Place biscuits on a baking sheet, sides touching.
  12. Bake for 12–14 minutes. (Optional: During the last 1–2 minutes, brush the tops lightly with melted butter and broil to brown the tops. Watch carefully.)
  13. As soon as biscuits come out of the oven, brush with melted butter. Once absorbed, brush again.
